01   About me

The story behind the screen

Growing up surrounded by the Himalayan peaks of Himachal Pradesh, I learned early that anything worth having requires patience and persistence. That same mountain mentality drives me toward programming — methodical, determined, always climbing.

I discovered coding as a way to build things that matter. What started as curiosity about how websites work has grown into a genuine passion for full-stack development — turning logic into living, breathing applications.

Currently pursuing my MCA at HPTU, I'm in the early chapters of what I know will be a long and rewarding journey. The best developers are those who never stop being students.
